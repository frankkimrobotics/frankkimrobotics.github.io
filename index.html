Differentiable packing simulation for 3D irrregular packing

  • Rapid growth of e-commerce sector in logistics domain requires advanced packing algorithm that considers irregular object shapes
  • However, existing methods (heuristics, RL) focus on accelerated collision checking between objects based on discrete shape representation
  • Fundamental challenges for e-commerce packing involves dynamic stability, object orientaiton in continuous space, and underexplored combinatorial search on object packing sequence
  • We addressed this challenges to convert packing problem into contiuous optimization using neural SDF, differentiable process, and topological graph search
  • We compared our methods with mixed-integer programming, heuristic methods, and RL-based policy and achieved the state-of-the-art
  • This work is collaboration with IMA, Italian logistics company

Uncertainty aware next-best-view planning for cluttered scene reconstruction with manipulability

  • Active reconstruction is necessary for embodied AI in real world environment with unseen and cluttered objects.
  • However, manipulation often fails due to the uncertainty in object shapes coming from high occlusion, sensor noise, and symmetry in object 6D pose
  • We address this problem by reconstructing object 3D shapes in neural SDF with shape uncertainty
  • For efficient planning, we've generated next-best-view planner for active reconstruction with robot manipulator considering industrial applications

Ice hockey player trajectory reconstruction from monocular video with GAN

  • Estimating player 3D trajectory is crucial for advanced sports analytics in career development, team strategy design, and injury analysis
  • However, sensor installation and attachment to athelets affects their performance and inefficient for scalable data collection
  • To address this limitation of data for machine learning, we've developed GAN-based image-to-image translation network for converting icerink to dense field of colorized points for camera estimation
  • For collected data of 1000 image samples of image frames and manually annotated camera pose, our method achieves 95% accuracy in terms of mIOU for partial and 92% of mIOU for whole ice rink

Autonomous medical crash cart for navigation in hospital environment

  • Autonomous medical crash cart is crucial for alleviating the burdens of medical nurses in hospital environment which is labor intensive, stressful, and emergencies
  • We converted a medical crash cart to autonomous by adding SW(YOLO,PID,ROS) and HW(Raspi, Intel Realsense D435, and ODROID with segway wheels)
  • We first desgined interaction diagram considering the basic functionalities of the crash cart following the nurse in indoor scenes
  • Our vision-based PID enabled to follow the target person within 1.4 meters and alleviating the burdens of carrying the heavy cart

  • Our method classifies and detect suspicious actions with X% accuracy

Training deep generative models with RL for Traveling Salesman Problem with unit circle neighborhoods

  • Traveling Salesman Problem(TSP) is one of the most popular NP-hard problems which is hard to find the general solution within a polynomial time once problem size increases
  • In robotic applications, complex task planning is not only NP-hard, but contains continuous variables that complicates the problem to NP-complete
  • Therefore, we focused on the traveling salesman problem with unit circle neighborhood where points in TSP are now continuous regions of unit circles
  • To solve this underexplored problem, we developed hybrid methods by training graph attention-based pointer network for TSP, and heuristic local optimization using virtual force method
  • Our method outperforms current SOTA heuristic solvers and efficiently converge to the local minima once TSP path is determined.

Automated camera-based estimation of rehabilitation criteria following ACL reconstruction

  • Rehabilitation process is hard to be measured due multiple factors including personalized performance, subjective metrics, and expensive installation of sensors
  • By using the stereo vision and human pose estimation algorithm, we reconstruct repetitive human motion during rehabilitation exercise that accurately predicts the acceleration of human hip joint motion
  • To overcome the inaccuracy from stereo images with estimated human skeleton poses, we've applied prinicpal component analysis (PCA), to reduce dimensions along the depth axis to reconstruct motion trajectory
  • Our method accurately estimates the force exerted by human with 98% which is validated by realtime load cell and rotary encoder data captured for validation
  • We've deployed this system to physicians with QT-based GUI, real time data streaming pipeline with tmux, and post-hoc processing with MATLAB and MySQL
